Physical Interpretation: Sepol Drive Speed (separator classifier speed) accounts for 61% of total predictive power across its variants. This is physically correct — the separator directly controls which particle sizes return to the mill for re-grinding vs. which pass through as finished product. The 2-4 hour lag reflects the time it takes for separated particles to complete the grinding circuit and appear in fineness measurements.
